Microsoft AI is a suite of artificial intelligence technologies developed by Microsoft to empower individuals and organizations. It includes tools like Azure AI, Copilot in Microsoft 365, and the Azure OpenAI Service, integrating advanced machine learning and natural language processing. Microsoft AI helps businesses automate tasks, analyze data, and build intelligent applications. It supports ethical AI development, emphasizing fairness, accountability, and transparency. From healthcare to finance, Microsoft AI enhances productivity, decision-making, and user experiences. With strong investments in research and partnerships, including with OpenAI, Microsoft continues to lead innovations in AI, making powerful capabilities accessible across industries.
